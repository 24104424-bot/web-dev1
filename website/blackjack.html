Blackjack Guide

The classic card game of skill and strategy

Objective

The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible without going over. It's a game that combines luck with strategy, making it one of the most popular casino games worldwide.

You're not competing against other players‚Äîonly against the dealer. This makes it an exciting one-on-one challenge.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value
  • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are all worth 10 points
  • Aces can be worth either 1 or 11‚Äîyour choice based on what helps your hand
  • A hand with an Ace valued as 11 is called a "soft" hand

Game Play

  • Each player receives two cards face up; dealer gets one up, one down
  • Hit: Request another card to improve your total
  • Stand: Keep your current hand and end your turn
  • Double Down: Double your bet and receive exactly one more card
  • Split: If you have two cards of the same value, split them into two hands
  • If your total exceeds 21, you "bust" and lose immediately

Winning Conditions

  • Your hand total is closer to 21 than the dealer's without busting
  • The dealer busts (goes over 21) while you don't
  • You get a "Blackjack" (Ace + 10/face card) which typically pays 3:2
  • If you and the dealer tie, it's a "push" and you get your bet back
