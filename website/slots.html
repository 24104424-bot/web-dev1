How Slots Work

Slots are chance-based games where you spin reels and try to match symbols along paylines.

Key Features

  • Paylines: winning symbol patterns.
  • Wilds: substitute for other symbols.
  • Scatters: trigger bonus rounds.
  • Jackpots: special big prizes.

Tips

Always set a budget. Look for slots with higher return-to-player (RTP) percentages.
