🥏 Frisbee Journey

My Frisbee Journey

🌟 The Beginning (2020)

March 2020

It all started on a beach in my hometown during the pandemic. My friends convinced me to try throwing the disc, and from that moment, I was hooked. The sound of the disc cutting through the air and the fun we shared made me realize this was something special.

"That first throw at the beach started it all — I never thought it would become my passion."

🏆 First Tournament (2022)

2022

My first tournament was a mix of excitement and nerves. I didn’t know what to expect, but it became one of the most unforgettable moments of my Frisbee journey. We gave our all, learned from every game, and built a stronger bond as a team.

  • Played 8 games over 2 days
  • Made my first layout catch
  • Met players from 15 different teams
  • Learned the importance of team communication

⚡ Experience Farming (2022-2023)

2022-2023

During this time, I focused on improving myself as a player. I joined training sessions, learned new strategies, and gained a lot of experience through different games and tournaments.

Key Milestones:

  1. Joined a competitive club team
  2. Mastered the forehand and backhand throws
  3. Practiced field awareness
  4. Traveled to many places and met new people
  5. Joined various tournaments across Mindanao

🌟 Present Day (2025)

2025

Now, Ultimate Frisbee has become a big part of my life and identity. I’ve grown so much since that first throw at the beach. I’m now the team captain of my university team and have competed in tournaments outside Mindanao, representing not just my school but also the community that helped me grow. Through every game, I’ve gained confidence in my playing style, learned to lead others, and built lasting friendships along the way. Frisbee has truly shaped who I am today — both on and off the field.
