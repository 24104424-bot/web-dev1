1. Backhand Throw

The most fundamental throw in Ultimate. Similar to a tennis backhand.

Steps:

  1. Grip the disc with your thumb on top, fingers underneath
  2. Stand sideways to your target
  3. Bring the disc across your body
  4. Snap your wrist as you release
  5. Follow through toward your target

Pro Tip: Keep your elbow close to your body for better control.

2. Forehand (Flick) Throw

A sidearm throw that allows for quick releases and different angles.

Steps:

  1. Use a two-finger grip (index and middle finger)
  2. Thumb on top of the disc
  3. Keep your wrist cocked back
  4. Snap forward with power from your wrist
  5. Release with spin for stability

3. Hammer Throw

An overhead throw that travels upside down and curves downward.

When to Use:

  • Going over a defender's head
  • Short to medium range passes
  • When throwing into the wind
  • Surprise element in your arsenal
