About Me

Who Am I?

Hi! I’m John Rene P. Panugan, and my Ultimate Frisbee journey began in 2020 during the pandemic at a beach in my hometown. My friends convinced me to play, and from that day, I fell in love with the sport.

My Frisbee journey grew even more when I came to Cebu to study at the University of San Carlos (USC). I started playing anywhere I could — in fields, parks, and open spaces — and met so many amazing people who share the same love for Ultimate. Cebu became special to me, not just because of my studies, but because it’s where I grew both as a player and as a person.

What is Ultimate Frisbee?

🎯 The Basics

Ultimate is a non-contact team sport played with a flying disc. Two teams of seven players compete to score goals by catching the disc in the opposing end zone.

🤝 Spirit of the Game

Ultimate is unique because it's self-officiated. Players are responsible for their own foul and line calls, emphasizing sportsmanship.

🌍 Global Community

Ultimate is played in over 80 countries with millions of players worldwide.
