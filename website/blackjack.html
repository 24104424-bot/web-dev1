Objective

The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Basic Rules

  • Number cards = face value.
  • Face cards = 10.
  • Ace = 1 or 11 (your choice).
  • Players can hit (draw a card) or stand (end their turn).
  • If you go over 21, you bust.
