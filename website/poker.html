♠️ Poker Guide

Objective

Poker combines luck, strategy, and skill. The aim is to create the best hand or convince others to fold.

Hand Rankings

  • Royal Flush (highest)
  • Straight Flush
  • Four of a Kind
  • Full House
  • Flush
  • Straight
  • Three of a Kind
  • Two Pair
  • One Pair
  • High Card (lowest)

Basic Play

Players are dealt cards and take turns betting. Rounds of betting are followed by revealing cards. Best hand wins the pot.
